Part One

I was sitting on a bench under a tree with my dad and brother.

Part Two

Waiting for something I can't remember. My brother and I, dying of boredom and curiosity.

Part Three

When we were young, my parents never let us on the internet or any device that had access to that world. We wanted to do one thing and one thing only. Watch youtube.

Part Four

After multiple minutes of begging and pleading and whining, my dad caves and opens the app. He opens the search bar and types in a video title. "Three Little Pigs - Silly Symphony Walt Disney 1933"

Part Five

The three of us sit on that bench under that tree watching the three little pigs. Twice.
